    What's New

    • New School Canteen

      Nutrition is very important at every stage. Fresh food is cooked everyday for the students who are unable to bring tiffin from their home. Our canteen is certified by FDA. Dal-rice is the favourite food of all the students which is served hot in the recess time. The food quality and hygiene is regularly checked before it is served to the students. We also practice organic farming on a small scale so that we can regularly provide fresh agricultural vegetables. How and what a child eats today can have a huge impact on their adolescence and adulthood.
